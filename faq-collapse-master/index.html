Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of incorporating your company in the Cayman Islands?

There are no direct taxes in the Cayman Islands, meaning that there is no corporate, income, sales or capital gains tax. This allows companies to keep more of their profits by minimizing their tax burder to zero.

What is the best type of company to incorporate in the Cayman Islands?

Regular companies that will operate in the Cayman Islands market and abroad should be set up as "Ordinary Resident Companies". Companies that don't serve the local Cayman market and only service foreign markets should be set up as "Exempt Companies".

How much does it cost to set up a company in the Cayman Islands?

It usually costs a couple thousand dollars in legal fees to incorporate a company in the Cayman Islands. It can be done for less if you don't outsource certain things to law firms, but most people don't have the expertise to do this, so they pay law firms to do it for them.
